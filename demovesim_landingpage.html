About VESIM

ParagraphInstituted in 1994 Vivekanand Education Society Institute of Management (VESIM) is one of the leading business schools in Mumbai. The Institute strives to create socially responsible business leaders by offering the best infrastructure, world-class faculty, transformative pedagogic practices, an interactive and experiential teaching methodology and a growing distinguished alumni network of professionals who are equipped to lead their business or organization in the future.

Program USP’s

- Around 8 Analytical Subjects covered in Business Analytics / Banking and Finance / Marketing / Human Resource Management / Operations and Supply Chain / International Business / Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning / Sports Management / Aviation Management
-Global Certification Course by SAS
-Opportunity to receive a Diploma in Banking & Finance (DBF) from Indian Institute of Banking & Finance (IIBF) and Master Certification from Society for Human Resource Management (SHRM)
-Opportunity of Double degree with International Institute like ZSEM – Croatia, UPCT – Spain and Lincoln University – USA
-International Study Tour to Cities like Munich, Salzburg, Vienna, Prague etc.
-Cutting Edge teaching on SAS software
-Communication, Soft Skills and Foreign Language course integrated with the program
-Industry-relevant electives offered across all domain.
